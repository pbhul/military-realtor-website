Northern Virginia Housing Market Information

The Northern Virginia housing market serves a significant military population. Market characteristics include:

  • Market Activity: Active market with regular inventory turnover
  • Price Ranges: Vary significantly by location and proximity to bases
  • Housing Types: Single-family homes, townhouses, condominiums, apartments available
  • Military-Friendly: Many landlords and sellers familiar with military relocations
  • VA Loan Acceptance: Area lenders experienced with VA loan processes
